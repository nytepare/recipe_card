Honey Orange Chicken

This is a sweet and tangy dish that I enjoy over rice that is healthy and still tastes good. I am a health advocate that still adores comfort food. This is the dish that pleases both the body and your stomach. If you like good food than this is the thing for you.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ honey
  • ½ concerntrated orange juice

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, orange juice, soy sauce, honey, garlic paste, ginger paste, and black pepper in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir until the sauce reduces to a sticky glaze and the chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  3. Place on a bed of rice and serve.

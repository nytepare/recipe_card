Honey Orange Chicken

This is a lovely sweet-and-sticky meal without the guilt the family whole family will enjoy. If you are cooking with rice or another side that takes awhile to cook, prepare that first. Taking 15 minutes to prep and 20 minutes to cook. It serves 2 and with 320 calories per serving.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ honey
  • ½ concerntrated orange juice

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, orange juice, soy sauce, honey, garlic paste, ginger paste, and black pepper in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir until the sauce reduces to a sticky glaze and the chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  3. Place on a bed of rice and serve.
